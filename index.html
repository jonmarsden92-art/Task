``` Explore the Beat Lab: Workflow & Sound Design Here's how to use the sequencer, shape the groove, and understand the built-in sounds. · 🎛️ Sequencer & Step Programming: The main grid is an 8-channel (Kick, Snare, HH, etc.) step sequencer. Click or drag on the cells to place hits. The vertical column highlights the current playhead position, and the top row shows the musical beat count (bars). You can toggle the entire row for a channel by clicking its name. · 🎚️ Playback & Groove Control: Hit the green ▶ PLAY button or press the Spacebar to start. Use the BPM controls to set the tempo (great for liquid or neuro vibes) and the SWING slider to add shuffle, which is essential for authentic drum & bass grooves. · 🎵 Presets & Sound Design: Load the Classic DNB, Liquid, Neurofunk, or Jungle presets to hear different pattern styles. Each channel generates its own synthesized sound (from a deep kick to a reese-style bass), with no external samples needed. You can also hit Random for inspiration or Clear to start from scratch.
